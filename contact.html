FAQ

Quick Answers

How do I track my order?
Once your order is shipped, we'll send a tracking link via WhatsApp and email. You can also reach out to us directly for updates.
Can I cancel my custom order?
Custom orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placing. After artwork has begun, cancellations are not possible but we offer free revision.
Do you ship internationally?
Currently we ship Pan India. International shipping is available on request with additional charges. Contact us to discuss.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept UPI/QR Code payments and Cash on Delivery. All UPI payments are secure and instant.
