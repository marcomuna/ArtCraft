Description

Our custom pencil portraits are created by a skilled artist using premium graphite and charcoal pencils on 200gsm acid-free paper. Every line is drawn by hand — no digital manipulation, no printing. The result is a genuinely unique artwork that captures the personality and emotion of your subject. Simply upload a clear reference photo and leave the rest to us.

The A4 size is perfect for framing and gifting, fitting standard frame sizes. Our A3 option is ideal for larger wall displays. Pair with any of our premium frames for a truly complete gift.
